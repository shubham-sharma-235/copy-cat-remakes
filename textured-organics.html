Remake Details:

Textured Organics Vol. 1 is a preset pack that pushes Vital to its limits. Revolving around Karplus-Strong synthesis, this pack brings organic emulations of real-life instruments and synthetic textures together, resulting in a distinctive and organic sound.

Each patch is designed following the same principle, using the same four macro knobs for easy and intuitive tweaking.

The signature feature of this collection is its dual-mode design, offering two presets within a single patch, with the ability to switch between them using the 'A/B' macro knob. With some patches even allowing for sound morphing, just like on the legendary Mellotron.

In total, this pack contains 23 instruments/sounds across 12 patches.
